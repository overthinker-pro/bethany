We Are Bethany Family

We are a small but deeply rooted family in Christ, growing together in the faith and teachings of the Malankara Orthodox Syrian Church. Our Sunday School is a place where children and youth are lovingly nurtured through prayer, Scripture, and tradition. Bound by grace and fellowship, we strive to live as disciples — learning, worshipping, and walking together in the light of Christ.

Our Purpose.

To lead our children into a life shaped by the love of Christ and the teachings of the Orthodox faith. We aim to nurture their spiritual growth through worship, learning, and meaningful community. By fostering a deep connection with God, we hope to prepare them for a life of service, compassion, and faithfulness.

Our Mission.

Our mission is to provide a spiritually enriching environment for the children entrusted to us. Through a blend of teachings, prayers, and Christian fellowship, we seek to instill the values of love, respect, and devotion. We are committed to guiding each child in their personal journey with Christ, ensuring that they grow in both faith and character, grounded in the teachings of the Holy Orthodox Church.
