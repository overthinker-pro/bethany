Welcome to Bethany

We are a small family of faithful believers, dedicated to guiding our children in the light of Orthodox Christian teachings. Every Sunday, we gather not just to teach, but to grow together — deepening our understanding of Christ and strengthening our walk with Him. Our Sunday School is built on love, scripture, and fellowship. Whether in prayer, song, or study, we aim to nurture a lifelong faith in the hearts of our young ones, shaping them to be steadfast and joyful followers of Christ.
